Gazpacho andaluz

Summer nectar
starter first course
lazy dinner

Ingredients

Rations are not important here. The more the better.

  • 6 ripe tomatoes
  • 2 green and 1 red peppers
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • 2 slice of bread
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Soak the bread in water
  2. Wash, peel and chop all the vegetables, removing the seeds from those that have them.
  3. Drain the bread by hand and place all the ingredients in a blender or food processor. Season with four tablespoons of oil and two tablespoons of vinegar.
  4. Mash well until it is a homogeneous puree and add cold water until you get a creamy texture.
  5. Then season with salt and pepper and set aside in the fridge. Serve the gazpacho very cold with toppings to taste. The traditional toppings are usually the same finely chopped vegetables used for the gazpacho, as well as croutons or small cubes of Serrano ham and grated boiled egg. Enjoy!
